About

We are a group of friends and colleagues who have all started travelling to Africa over the last few years for both business and pleasure.

We all quickly realized that there was a shortage of online information available about African cities, and no single source of information, advice and recommendations for the discerning traveller.

With new hotels, restaurants, bars, shops, galleries and other attractions appearing every day in cities across the continent, we decided to create a website which met our own needs and which we could also share with other travellers to African cities.

We do hope you find our website useful and enjoyable and that you will engage us with your comments and recommendations.
